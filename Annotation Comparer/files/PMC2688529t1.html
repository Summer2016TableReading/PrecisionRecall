Prdx1−/−MEFs and Prdx1+/+MEFs were retrovirally infected with constructs carrying genes for H-RasV12 or ErbB-2/neuT (ErbB-2Val644Glu) and treated after plating in soft agar either with 10 or 25 μM Ly294002 every 2 days or Wortmannin 5 or 10 nM every day. Colonies were counted after 21 days. Decrease in colony formation (%) relates to untreated of the same genotype (%). This experiment is representative of three independent studies with two different sets of MEF clones from Prdx1 littermates.
